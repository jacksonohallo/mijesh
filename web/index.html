About Us

About Us

Mijesh Enterprises Limited is a professional building and construction firm incorporated in Kenya under companies Act CAP 486 to respond to the increasing demands of building and construction services in Kenya. Our prime focus is to provide building and construction services towards building works, civil engineering, telecommunication and power works.
Over the years the company has enjoyed continuous, steady growth as a result of its impeccable reputation for integrity, high standards of workmanship, use of resource efficient construction technique, innovation and highly durable construction technologies and comprehensive on site safety measures.
All the projects completed within time and cost, accumulated experience and business principles adopted over the years have established Mijesh in the distinguished class

Our Services

Civil Works

Mijesh Enterprises Limited is able to undertake bulk earthworks, heavy concrete works, large paved areas, access roads and piled foundations as well as supporting civil engineering enabling works for oil, gas and geothermal drilling such as access roads, well pads, airstrips and construction camps.

Building Projects

From Industrial Developments to Public Utilities, from Commercial Buildings to Residential Projects, Mijesh Enterprises offers services encompassing everything inside a building which makes it safe and comfortable to be in.

Electrical & lightning installation

Over time Mijesh has undertaken and commissioned projects of varied nature from Industrial Developments to Public Utilities, from Commercial Buildings to Large Civil Engineering Projects. Mijesh Enterprises has handled projects across varied regions and markets and is operational in Kenya with prospects for the greater Eastern Africa region.

Power Infrastructure

Sufficient power supply is critical for industrialization of economies. We seek to contribute to realization of the same by being a key partner in the region offering world class services in construction of power stations, substations and transmission lines of upto 400kV.

Telecommunications

Our experience in the region coupled with dynamic engineering and project management skills means we are able to deliver cost-effective and innovative solutions to complex civil engineering problems.

Power Works

Keeping in view of the experience, expertise, financial resources and plant & equipment, we are capable and confident to undertake any project of substantial magnitude within our areas of focus.
